THE VISION

Mumbai is a city of shadows and light. Through the lens of my Sony a6400, I seek the quiet moments amidst the chaos—the hazy docks at dawn, the lonely street corners, and the stories that often go unnoticed. My work is an exploration of the cinematic side of everyday life.
