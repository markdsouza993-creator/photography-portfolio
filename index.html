About the Artist

Based in Malad, Mumbai, my journey is defined by a late start but a rapid obsession. I’ve always watched the world with a photographer's eye, but only recently picked up the camera to prove it.

I believe you don't need the most expensive gear to tell a story. I am a student of the streets, constantly learning to freeze time—turning the chaos of Mumbai into cinematic silence.
