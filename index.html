About & Gear

Mark Dsouza
Visual Storyteller · Malad, Mumbai
"I am a student of the streets — learning to freeze time, turning Mumbai's chaos into cinematic silence."

Based in Malad, Mumbai, my journey is defined by a late start but a rapid obsession. I've always watched the world with a photographer's eye — only recently picked up the camera to prove it.

I believe you don't need the most expensive gear to tell a story. Every frame is a negotiation between what I see and what the street allows me to capture.
